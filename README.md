## What Is Dripshipper?

Dripshipper is a private-label coffee dropshipping platform based in San Diego, California. The concept is simple: you build the brand, they handle the coffee.

When a customer places an order in your store, Dripshipper roasts the beans fresh — often the same day — packages them with your logo and label, and ships directly to your customer within 3–5 business days. Your customer never sees Dripshipper's name. From their point of view, the coffee came straight from you.

It connects with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Wix, and Squarespace. Once set up, the whole fulfillment process runs automatically. You're not printing labels, packing boxes, or touching a single bag of coffee.

The catalog currently includes 70+ products: single-origin beans, blends, flavored coffees, light/medium/dark roasts, whole bean, ground, pods, instant coffee, and tea. You can build a full product lineup without ever needing a second supplier.

## The Honest Trade-offs

No product is perfect, and Dripshipper isn't either. A few things to be aware of:

**Margins require strategy.** The subscription fee plus per-unit wholesale cost means you can't race to the bottom on pricing. You need to build a real brand with real positioning. That's actually fine — it filters out the people trying to flip commodity coffee — but if you expected to undercut Amazon on price, that math won't work here.

**Marketing is on you.** Dripshipper handles everything behind the scenes beautifully. It doesn't drive traffic to your store. Social media, email, SEO, ads — that's your job. The platform gives you infrastructure; you bring the audience.

**Support can have delays.** Most reviews praise the support team. A minority mention slower response times during busy periods. Worth knowing, not a dealbreaker.
